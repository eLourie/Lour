Science History Notes

Penicillin, the first antibiotic, was discovered by Alexander Fleming in 1928
when he noticed mould killing bacteria on a culture plate.

Albert Einstein received the 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of
the photoelectric effect, not for relativity.

The Eiffel Tower in Paris was designed by Gustave Eiffel and completed in 1889
for the World's Fair. It stands 330 metres tall.

The GDPR, a European Union regulation on data protection and privacy, took
effect in 2018.
